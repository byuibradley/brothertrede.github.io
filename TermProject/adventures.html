THE ROOKIE - $199 per 2 hours

So you're the day dreamer. Sitting in your office cubicle, tired of sifting through all of the stacks of paperwork and just want to get away. You want something that unleashes a little bit of adventure, yet you still consider yourself a little timid. You dedice you'd like to go rafting, but have never done it before. Our guides will spend 1-2 hours going over all of the equipment, manuevers, and safety needs. We'll get you into the water and off we go. As you head down the river and find yourself nearing a complex rapid, your guide will coach everyone in the raft as you work together. The experience will be exilerating and enjoyable!

THE PRO - $299 per 4 hours

You have been in the rivers a few times and love a good rocking back and forth as the raft hits the rapids. You find yourself in constant enjoyment from the constant environmental changes from calm to heavy rapids and back again. You sometimes might seem that you are in over your head a little bit, but with the use of our experienced guides, they will be there to coach everyone when the water gets rough. The adrenaline will be pounding and the happiness and excitment from succeeding will keep asking you for more. Our Pro package is just for you!

THE VETERAN - $429 per 8 hours

They call you the veteran of the white water rapids. You have been rafting tens if not hundreds of times and every time you go rafting, you are expecting a little more. While we are always cautious about water levels and weather conditions, our Veteran package is a full-day adventure. While our Rookie and Pro packages are only half or quarter day affairs, this veteran package is not for the light hearted. It will try your emotions, wear your strength, but give you the most satisfaction as you achieve 6 to 7 hours of being on the water. You will need to bring your A-Game. We'll gladly bring an guide, but it's going to be up to you to determine the team. They will recommend aspects for safety, but you're the coach on this package! Raft on!
