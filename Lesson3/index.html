Site Name

Roll The Dice Weather

Purpose:

The purpose of Roll The Dice Weather is to provide a clever Name for a weather website, but provide as accurate weather as possible. We all know that sometimes when the weatherman says it's going to snow and it does not, we think are they just rolling some dice? The website will provide users with the ability to check weather at various locations, obtain access to a radar, and a 7-day forecast. Clean and simple!
